## Notes

Slice the vegetables thinly so they cook through quickly and hold together well in the pancakes.

Flatten the pancakes with a spatula after adding them to the pan to create thin, crispy pancakes.

Transfer the cooked pancakes to a paper towel-lined plate to absorb excess oil.

Serve hot with the dipping sauce.

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and kosher salt.
2. Add the water and mix until a smooth batter forms.
3. Add the leek greens, carrot, scallions, red cabbage, and jalapeño.
4. Mix until the vegetables are evenly coated in the batter.
5. In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, lemon juice, water, sesame oil, and sesame seeds. Set aside.
6. Heat the avocado oil in a large frying pan over medium heat.
7. Scoop approximately 1/4 cup of batter into the pan for each pancake.
8. Flatten each pancake slightly with a spatula.
9. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the bottom is golden brown and crispy.
10. Flip and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
11. Transfer the cooked pancakes to a paper towel-lined plate to absorb excess oil.
12. Repeat with the remaining batter.
13. Serve hot with the dipping sauce.

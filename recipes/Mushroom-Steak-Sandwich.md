## Notes

The mushrooms can be marinated for anywhere from 30 minutes to 2 hours. A longer marinating time allows the mushrooms to absorb more flavor.

Pressing the lion's mane mushrooms while cooking removes moisture, creates a denser texture, and helps develop a golden-brown crust.

Use vegan ciabatta and crispy onions if you want to keep the entire sandwich vegan.

## Instructions

1. In a large tray, combine the beetroot juice, dark soy sauce, smoked paprika, maple syrup or agave, olive oil, brown miso paste, salt, and garlic powder. Mix well to make the marinade.
2. Heat a small amount of oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
3. Add the lion's mane mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes on each side.
4. As the mushrooms soften, gradually press them down with a spatula or another heavy pan to release moisture and flatten them into steak-like pieces.
5. Continue cooking and pressing until the mushrooms are golden brown on both sides.
6. Transfer the mushrooms to the marinade and turn to coat thoroughly. Marinate for 30 minutes to 2 hours.
7. Meanwhile, combine the wholegrain mustard, vegan mayonnaise, and chopped parsley in a small bowl.
8. Slice and toast the ciabatta bread if desired.
9. Heat a small amount of oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat.
10. Remove the mushrooms from the marinade and fry until deeply browned and charred around the edges.
11. Spread the mustard mayonnaise onto the ciabatta.
12. Add the rocket, charred lion's mane mushrooms, and crispy onions.
13. Close the sandwich and serve immediately.

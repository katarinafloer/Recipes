## Notes

Marinate the tofu in soy sauce for 30 minutes before assembling the wraps.

Do not soak the rice paper for too long. Dip it briefly in water so it remains slightly firm and becomes easier to wrap as it softens.

Avoid overfilling the wraps, as this can make them difficult to seal and more likely to burst while air frying.

## Instructions

1. Cut the tofu into strips and marinate in soy sauce for 30 minutes.
2. Prepare the sushi rice according to package instructions and allow it to cool slightly.
3. Thinly slice the cucumber and carrots.
4. Dip one rice paper wrapper briefly in water and place it on a clean, flat surface.
5. Place a piece of nori in the center of the rice paper.
6. Add a layer of sushi rice, cucumber, carrots, and marinated tofu.
7. Place another piece of nori on top of the filling.
8. Fold the sides of the rice paper inward and tightly wrap around the filling.
9. Brush or lightly coat the outside of the wrap with sesame oil.
10. Sprinkle sesame seeds over the outside and gently press them onto the rice paper.
11. Repeat with the remaining ingredients.
12. Place the wraps in the air fryer basket in a single layer, leaving space between each wrap.
13. Air fry at 350°F (175°C) for 8-10 minutes, or until golden and crispy.
14. Allow to cool slightly before serving.

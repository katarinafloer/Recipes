Baked Eggs with Mushrooms and Goat Cheese Cream

Breakfast · 15 minutes

Source

Ingredients

  • 1 egg per ramekin
  • 150 g fresh goat cheese
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 100 g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

Not logged yet.

Notes

Bake the eggs in a water bath to ensure gentle, even cooking.

The eggs are ready when the whites are set but the yolks are still soft and runny. Adjust the cooking time depending on your preferred yolk consistency.

Serve immediately with toasted bread for dipping.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. In a small bowl, mash the fresh goat cheese with the heavy cream until smooth and creamy. Refrigerate until needed.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat and sauté the mushrooms with the garlic and chopped parsley until the mushrooms are tender and lightly browned.
  4. Divide the goat cheese cream among oven-safe ramekins.
  5. Add the sautéed mushrooms on top of the goat cheese cream.
  6. Crack one egg into each ramekin.
  7. Place the ramekins in a deep baking dish.
  8. Carefully pour hot water into the baking dish until it reaches approximately halfway up the sides of the ramekins.
  9. Transfer the baking dish to the oven and bake for about 10 minutes, or until the egg whites are set and the yolks are cooked to your liking.
  10. Carefully remove the ramekins from the water bath and serve immediately.

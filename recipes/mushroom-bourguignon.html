Mushroom Bourguignon

Dinner · 15 minutes

Source

Ingredients

  • 1 kg mixed mushrooms (button mushrooms, oyster mushrooms, portobello mushrooms, or a combination), sliced
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 80 g butter
  • 500 ml vegetable stock
  • 200 ml red wine
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 sprig rosemary
  • 2 sprigs thyme
  • 1 bay leaf

Date Cooked

Not logged yet.

Notes

Use a mixture of mushrooms for a greater variety of textures and flavors.

Allow the mushrooms to cook down and release their moisture before adding the flour and liquids.

Serve with mashed potatoes, polenta, pasta, or crusty bread.

Instructions

  1. Finely chop the onion and shallot. Slice the carrots and mushrooms.
  2. Melt the butter in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  3. Add the onion, shallot, and carrots and cook for 5-6 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the mushrooms and continue cooking until they have released their moisture and reduced in volume.
  5. Sprinkle the flour over the vegetables and stir well to coat everything evenly.
  6. Add the vegetable stock and red wine, stirring well to prevent lumps from forming.
  7. Add the rosemary, thyme, and bay leaf.
  8. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.
  9. Simmer uncovered for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender and the sauce has thickened.
  10. Remove the rosemary stems, thyme stems, and bay leaf.
  11. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.
  12. Serve hot with mashed potatoes or your preferred side.

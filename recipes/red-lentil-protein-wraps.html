Red Lentil Protein Wraps

Lunch · 1 hour 10 minutes · 5 wraps

Source

Ingredients

  • 150 g dry split red lentils
  • 360 ml warm water
  • 1 handful spinach
  • 2-3 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1 pinch salt
  • oil, for cooking (optional)

Date Cooked

Not logged yet.

Notes

Each wrap contains approximately 9 g of protein and 112 calories, depending on the ingredients and brands used.

The lentils should soak for at least 1 hour before blending.

The batter will thicken as it sits. Blend it for a few seconds between cooking wraps if needed to restore a smooth consistency.

A high-quality non-stick pan may not require any oil.

Instructions

  1. Rinse the red lentils thoroughly under running water.
  2. Place the lentils in a bowl with the warm water and soak for at least 1 hour.
  3. Transfer the lentils and soaking water to a blender.
  4. Add the spinach, nutritional yeast, and salt.
  5. Blend for about 2 minutes, or until the batter is completely smooth and lump-free.
  6. Heat a small amount of oil in a non-stick pan or skillet. If using a high-quality non-stick pan, the oil can be omitted.
  7. Reduce the heat to medium-low and pour enough batter into the pan to create a thin layer.
  8. Use the back of a spoon to spread the batter evenly across the surface of the pan if necessary.
  9. Cook for 3-4 minutes, or until the edges begin to lift away from the pan.
  10. Flip the wrap and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  11. Transfer the cooked wrap to a plate and repeat with the remaining batter.
  12. If the batter thickens while sitting, blend it for a few seconds before cooking the next wrap.
  13. Fill the wraps with your preferred protein, vegetables, sauces, and other toppings.

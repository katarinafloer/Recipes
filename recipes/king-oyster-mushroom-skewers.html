King Oyster Mushroom Skewers

Snack · 10 minutes

Source

Ingredients

  • 1 king oyster mushroom, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili crisp
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • salt, for boiling
  • oil, for cooking
  • sesame seeds, for serving

Date Cooked

Notes

Slice the king oyster mushroom lengthwise into thin, even pieces so they cook quickly and can be easily threaded onto skewers.

Soaking wooden skewers in water before cooking can help prevent them from burning.

Adjust the amount of chili crisp depending on your preferred spice level.

Instructions

  1. Thinly slice the king oyster mushroom lengthwise.
  2. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.
  3. Add the mushroom slices and boil for 2 minutes.
  4. Drain thoroughly and allow the mushrooms to cool slightly.
  5. Thread the mushroom slices onto skewers.
  6. Heat a small amount of oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat.
  7. Add the mushroom skewers and pan-fry until golden brown on both sides.
  8. In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, chili crisp, and sesame oil.
  9. Pour or brush the sauce over the mushroom skewers and cook briefly until the mushrooms are evenly coated and the sauce has caramelized slightly.
  10. Transfer to a plate, garnish with sesame seeds, and serve hot.

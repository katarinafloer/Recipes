Chicken and Leek Soup with Parmesan Dumplings

Lunch/Dinner · 30 minutes · 4

Source

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 pound leeks, white and light green parts only, halved lengthwise, sliced 1/2 inch thick, and thoroughly washed
  • 1 teaspoon table salt, divided
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 ounce Parmesan cheese, grated (about 1/2 cup)
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3 cups shredded rotisserie chicken
  • fresh parsley, chopped, for serving (optional)

Notes

Use only the white and light green parts of the leeks and wash them thoroughly after slicing to remove any dirt trapped between the layers.

Do not stir the dumplings during the first 2 minutes of cooking so they have time to set.

Pecorino Romano can be substituted for Parmesan.

For easier preparation, trim, slice, and wash the leeks, grate the Parmesan, and shred the rotisserie chicken in advance.

Garnish with chopped fresh parsley if desired.

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter in a Dutch oven or large pot over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced leeks and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  3. Cook for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the leeks are softened and beginning to brown.
  4. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute, or until fragrant.
  5. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a simmer.
  6. Reduce the heat to medium.
  7. In a medium bowl, combine the flour, grated Parmesan, water, beaten egg, remaining 1/2 teaspoon salt, baking powder, and black pepper.
  8. Mix until a thick dumpling dough forms.
  9. Using two spoons, scrape rough tablespoon-sized portions of dumpling dough directly into the simmering soup.
  10. Cook the dumplings without stirring for 2 minutes.
  11. Gently stir to separate any dumplings that have stuck together.
  12. Continue cooking for another 2 minutes.
  13. Carefully stir in the shredded rotisserie chicken.
  14. Cook for about 1 minute, or until the chicken is heated through.
  15. Divide among bowls, garnish with chopped parsley if desired, and serve hot.

## Notes

Vodka creates a clean, citrus-forward cocktail, gin adds botanical notes, and bourbon creates a richer and warmer drink.

To make honey syrup, combine equal parts honey and warm water and stir until completely dissolved.

For a mocktail version, omit the alcohol and add additional soda water or a non-alcoholic spirit.

Double-straining removes the muddled clementine pulp, seeds, and pieces of thyme from the finished cocktail.

## Instructions

1. Add the clementine, thyme sprigs, lemon juice, honey syrup, and vodka, gin, or bourbon to a cocktail shaker.
2. Muddle thoroughly to release the clementine juice and the aroma from the thyme.
3. Fill the cocktail shaker with ice.
4. Shake vigorously until well chilled.
5. Fill a serving glass with fresh ice.
6. Double-strain the cocktail into the prepared glass.
7. Top with soda water.
8. Garnish with a fresh clementine slice and a sprig of thyme.
9. Serve immediately.

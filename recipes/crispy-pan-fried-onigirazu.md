## Notes

Fair warning: regular tuna sandwiches are never going to hit the same after this.

## Instructions

1. Mix tuna with mayo, Sriracha, salt, and pepper. Set aside.
2. Fry the eggs and prep all fillings before building.
3. Dip 2 rice paper sheets in warm water, then overlap them on a cutting board. Layer 2 nori sheets on top.
4. Cut 2 slits to create 4 sections. Place rice in one section, tuna across from it, egg in the third, and avocado and cucumber in the fourth.
5. Fold rice over avocado, fold that stack onto the tuna, fold tuna over the egg, fold egg over to the rice, then fold rice over cucumber.
6. Wrap the whole square in 2 more wet rice paper sheets, press to seal, and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
7. Pan fry in neutral oil on medium-high heat, searing all sides until golden and crispy.
8. Slice in half.

Spinach and Cannellini Bean Gnocchi

Dinner · 10 minutes

Source

Ingredients

  • 1 package precooked gnocchi
  • 3 frozen cubes spinach or approximately 250 g fresh spinach
  • 1/2 can cannellini beans, with some of their liquid
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red chili, chopped
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • olive oil, for cooking
  • vegan stracciatella or other soft cheese, for serving
  • lemon zest, for serving
  • additional chopped chili, for serving
  • additional olive oil, for serving

Notes

Blend the cannellini beans with some of their liquid to create a smooth and creamy sauce without adding cream.

If using frozen spinach, thaw it before blending. If using fresh spinach, wilt it briefly before blending for a smoother sauce.

Adjust the amount of bean liquid as needed to reach your preferred sauce consistency.

Instructions

  1. Add the spinach, cannellini beans with some of their liquid, lemon juice, salt, and black pepper to a blender.
  2. Blend until completely smooth and creamy, adding additional bean liquid or water if needed. Set aside.
  3. Heat a generous drizzle of olive oil in a large frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Add the minced garlic and chopped chili and sauté for 1-2 minutes, or until fragrant.
  5. Add the precooked gnocchi directly to the pan.
  6. Cook the gnocchi, stirring occasionally, until golden brown and lightly crispy on the outside.
  7. Pour the spinach and cannellini bean sauce into the pan.
  8. Stir until the gnocchi are evenly coated.
  9. Simmer for 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce is heated through and has thickened slightly.
  10. Taste and adjust the seasoning with additional salt, black pepper, or lemon juice as needed.
  11. Divide among bowls and top with vegan stracciatella.
  12. Finish with lemon zest, chopped chili, and a generous drizzle of olive oil.

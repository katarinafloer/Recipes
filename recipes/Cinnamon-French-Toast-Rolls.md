## Notes

Soft sandwich bread works best because it is easier to flatten and roll without tearing.

The cinnamon butter should be spread in a thin layer to prevent it from leaking excessively while cooking.

Nutella, powdered sugar, and strawberries are optional toppings.

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, combine the melted butter, brown sugar, and 1/2 tablespoon cinnamon to make the cinnamon butter.
2. In a separate shallow bowl, whisk together the egg, milk, and vanilla extract.
3. In another small bowl, combine the granulated sugar and remaining 1/2 tablespoon cinnamon to make the cinnamon sugar.
4. Cut the crusts from the slices of bread.
5. Flatten each slice of bread with a rolling pin or the side of a drinking glass.
6. Spread a thin layer of cinnamon butter over each slice of bread.
7. Roll each slice tightly into a cylinder.
8. Dip each roll into the egg and milk mixture, coating all sides.
9. Melt a small amount of butter in a frying pan over medium heat.
10. Add the rolls and cook, turning regularly, until all sides are golden brown.
11. Remove the rolls from the pan and coat with the cinnamon sugar while still warm.
12. Drizzle with Nutella and sprinkle with powdered sugar if desired.
13. Garnish with sliced strawberries and serve warm.

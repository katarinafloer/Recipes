Phyllo Feta Cigars

Snack · 35 minutes · 20-24 cigars

Source

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (12 oz) feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons fresh chives, chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 10-12 sheets phyllo dough, thawed if frozen
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • honey, for serving (optional)

Notes

Keep unused phyllo dough covered with a lightly damp kitchen towel while assembling the cigars to prevent it from drying out.

Place the filling along the short edge of each phyllo rectangle for crispier cigars.

For a vegan version, substitute plant-based cheese for the feta, use an appropriate egg substitute or omit the eggs if the cheese mixture holds together, and use olive oil or melted plant-based butter instead of butter.

Serve warm with a drizzle of honey if desired.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the crumbled feta, eggs, dill, chives, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Mix until the filling is evenly combined.
  4. Place one sheet of phyllo dough on a clean work surface and brush lightly with melted butter.
  5. Place a second sheet of phyllo directly on top and brush lightly with melted butter.
  6. Cut the layered phyllo sheets into four evenly sized rectangles.
  7. Place approximately 1 tablespoon of feta filling along the short edge of each rectangle, leaving about 1/2 inch of space on each side.
  8. Roll the phyllo over the filling, then fold the sides inward halfway through rolling.
  9. Continue rolling tightly to form a cigar shape.
  10. Repeat with the remaining phyllo dough and filling.
  11. Place the cigars seam side down on a baking sheet.
  12. Brush the tops lightly with additional melted butter.
  13. Bake for 30 minutes, or until deeply golden brown and crispy.
  14. Allow to cool slightly and serve warm with honey if desired.

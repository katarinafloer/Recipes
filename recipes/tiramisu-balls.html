Tiramisu Balls

Dessert · 30 minutes · 10 balls

Source

Ingredients

  • 250 g mascarpone
  • 100 g ladyfinger biscuits
  • 1 egg, separated
  • 2 teaspoons coffee
  • 1 packet vanilla sugar
  • unsweetened cocoa powder, for coating

Date Cooked

Not logged yet.

Notes

Chill the tiramisu mixture before coating if it is too soft to shape easily.

Keep the tiramisu balls refrigerated until ready to serve.

This recipe contains raw egg. Use a pasteurized egg if serving to pregnant people, young children, older adults, or anyone with a weakened immune system.

Instructions

  1. Separate the egg white from the yolk.
  2. Beat the egg white until stiff peaks form.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolk with the vanilla sugar until the mixture becomes pale and creamy.
  4. Add the mascarpone to the egg yolk mixture and mix until smooth.
  5. Gently fold the beaten egg white into the mascarpone mixture.
  6. Add the coffee and gently mix until incorporated.
  7. Finely crush or blend the ladyfinger biscuits.
  8. Add the crushed ladyfingers to the mascarpone mixture and mix by hand until evenly combined.
  9. Shape the mixture into 10 balls, or into balls of your preferred size.
  10. Place the tiramisu balls in the refrigerator for 15 minutes to firm up.
  11. Add the unsweetened cocoa powder to a small bowl.
  12. Roll each chilled tiramisu ball in the cocoa powder until completely coated.
  13. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

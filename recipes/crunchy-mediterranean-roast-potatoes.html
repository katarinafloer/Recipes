Crunchy Mediterranean Roast Potatoes

Side Dish · 20 minutes

Source

Ingredients

  • 4 potatoes, peeled and cut into cubes
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon honey, plus more for serving
  • 1 block feta cheese, crumbled
  • vegetable oil, for roasting

Notes

Parboiling the potatoes and shaking them after draining roughens the edges, helping them become extra crispy in the oven.

Preheating the vegetable oil in the baking tray helps the potatoes begin crisping as soon as they are added.

The lemon, oregano, and honey sauce can also be used as a dressing for salads, pasta, or other roasted vegetables.

Instructions

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into evenly sized cubes.
  2. Place the potatoes in a pot, cover with water, add a sprinkle of salt, and bring to a boil. Cook for 15 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  4. During the last 5 minutes of boiling the potatoes, pour enough vegetable oil into a deep baking tray to just cover the bottom. Place the tray in the oven for 5 minutes to heat the oil.
  5. Meanwhile, combine the lemon zest, lemon juice, olive oil, oregano, chili flakes, salt, and honey in a small bowl. Mix well and set aside.
  6. Drain the potatoes thoroughly and shake them vigorously in the pot or colander to roughen and fluff the edges.
  7. Carefully add the potatoes to the baking tray with the hot oil and turn them to coat evenly.
  8. Roast for 20 minutes, then remove the tray from the oven and turn the potatoes.
  9. Return to the oven for another 20 minutes. Remove and turn the potatoes again.
  10. Roast for another 20 minutes, then remove the tray and turn the potatoes once more.
  11. Drizzle half of the lemon, oregano, and honey sauce over the potatoes and toss well to coat.
  12. Return the potatoes to the oven for 5 minutes.
  13. Allow the potatoes to cool slightly, then sprinkle generously with crumbled feta.
  14. Drizzle with additional honey and serve with the remaining sauce as desired.

## Notes

Roasting the butternut squash and feta before blending gives the soup a deeper, richer flavor.

Adjust the amount of vegetable stock depending on your preferred soup consistency.

The feta crisp can be prepared while the soup is simmering.

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 190°C (375°F).
2. Peel and cut the butternut squash into cubes.
3. Place the butternut squash and block of feta on a baking tray.
4. Drizzle with olive oil and roast for 20-25 minutes, or until the butternut squash is tender and lightly browned.
5. Transfer the roasted butternut squash and feta to a large pot.
6. Add the vegetable stock and simmer for a few minutes.
7. Blend until completely smooth and creamy. Add additional vegetable stock if needed to reach your preferred consistency.
8. Reduce the oven temperature to 150°C (300°F).
9. To prepare the feta crisp, place additional feta in a bowl with the za'atar and a drizzle of olive oil.
10. Mash until combined, then spread the mixture into a thin layer on a parchment-lined baking tray.
11. Bake for 15 minutes, or until lightly golden and crisp around the edges.
12. Allow the feta crisp to cool slightly, then break into pieces.
13. Serve the soup in bowls topped with the feta crisp, fresh dill, and chili oil.

Scissor-Cut Pasta

Dinner · 15 minutes · 1

Source

Ingredients

  • 100 g all-purpose flour or 00 flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 pinch salt
  • salt, for boiling water

Date Cooked

Not logged yet.

Notes

Resting the dough for 10 minutes is optional, but it will make the pasta slightly softer and easier to cut.

For one serving, the pasta can be cut directly into the boiling water.

When making multiple servings, cut the pasta pieces onto a floured board first so they can all be added to the boiling water at approximately the same time.

Finish cooking the pasta directly in your preferred sauce.

Instructions

  1. Place the flour, egg, and salt in a bowl or on a clean work surface.
  2. Mix until a rough dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough until soft and smooth.
  4. Optionally, cover the dough and let it rest for 10 minutes while preparing your pasta sauce.
  5. Bring a pot of generously salted water to a boil.
  6. Hold the dough over the boiling water and use clean kitchen scissors to snip small pieces directly into the pot.
  7. Boil the pasta for 3-4 minutes, or until cooked to your preferred texture.
  8. Transfer the pasta directly to your sauce of choice.
  9. Toss the pasta with the sauce and cook briefly until well coated.
  10. Serve immediately.

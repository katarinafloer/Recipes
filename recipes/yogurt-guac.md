## Notes

Greek yogurt makes the guacamole creamier and adds a little extra protein.

Adjust the amount of lemon juice, salt, and black pepper to taste.

For a chunkier guacamole, mash the avocado lightly and gently fold in the remaining ingredients.

## Instructions

1. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
2. Mash the avocado with a fork to your preferred consistency.
3. Add the diced tomato and Greek yogurt.
4. Drizzle with lemon juice and season with salt and black pepper.
5. Mix until combined.
6. Taste and adjust the lemon juice, salt, and black pepper as needed.
7. Serve immediately.

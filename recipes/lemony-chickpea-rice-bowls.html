Lemony Chickpea Rice Bowls

Lunch · 25 min · 4

Ingredients

  • chickpeas
  • rice
  • spinach
  • lemon
  • cucumber
  • feta

Date Cooked

  • 2026-07-08

Notes

Keep crunchy vegetables separate until serving. Good with extra lemon and black pepper.

Steps

  1. Cook rice.
  2. Warm chickpeas with olive oil, lemon, salt, and pepper.
  3. Assemble with spinach, cucumber, feta, and sauce.

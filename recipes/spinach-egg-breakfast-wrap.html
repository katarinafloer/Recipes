Spinach Egg Breakfast Wrap

Breakfast · 12 min · 1

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • spinach
  • tortilla
  • cheddar
  • hot sauce

Date Cooked

  • 2026-07-09

Notes

Good freezer option if wrapped tightly. Reheat in a skillet for the best texture.

Steps

  1. Scramble eggs with spinach.
  2. Add cheddar and hot sauce.
  3. Wrap tightly in a tortilla.

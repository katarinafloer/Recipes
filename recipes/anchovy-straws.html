Anchovy Straws

Snack · 20 minutes

Source

Ingredients

  • 2 sheets puff pastry
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tin anchovies
  • Aleppo pepper
  • Parmesan, finely grated

Date Cooked

Not logged yet.

Notes

Keep the puff pastry cold while working with it. Freezing the assembled pastry for 15 minutes makes it easier to cut clean, even straws.

Instructions

  1. Brush one side of both sheets of puff pastry with beaten egg.
  2. Arrange the anchovies from the entire tin in three evenly spaced ribbons across one sheet of puff pastry.
  3. Sprinkle with Aleppo pepper and finely grated Parmesan.
  4. Place the second sheet of puff pastry on top, egg-washed side facing down, to create a sandwich.
  5. Transfer to the freezer for 15 minutes to firm up.
  6. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  7. Remove the pastry from the freezer and cut into 1 cm wide straws.
  8. Place the straws on their sides on the lined baking tray.
  9. Bake for 15 minutes, or until puffed, crisp, and golden brown.
  10. Allow to cool slightly before serving.

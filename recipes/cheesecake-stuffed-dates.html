Cheesecake Stuffed Dates

Dessert · 20 minutes · 12 dates

Source

Ingredients

  • 12 Medjool dates
  • 3 tablespoons mascarpone cheese
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 Lotus Biscoff biscuits, crushed
  • 200 g white chocolate, melted

Notes

Make sure the dates are completely dry before filling and coating them so the melted chocolate sticks properly.

Keep the cheesecake dates refrigerated until ready to serve.

Instructions

  1. Wash and thoroughly dry the dates.
  2. Slice each date lengthwise and remove the pit, keeping the date intact.
  3. Set aside 1 tablespoon of the crushed Lotus Biscoff biscuits for decorating.
  4. In a small bowl, combine the mascarpone, remaining crushed biscuits, and vanilla extract.
  5. Transfer the mascarpone mixture to a piping bag or use a small spoon to fill the center of each date.
  6. Gently press the dates closed around the filling.
  7. Melt the white chocolate until smooth.
  8. Dip each filled date into the melted white chocolate until completely coated.
  9. Place the chocolate-coated dates on a parchment-lined tray.
  10. Sprinkle with the reserved crushed Lotus Biscoff biscuits before the chocolate sets.
  11. Refrigerate until the chocolate is firm and keep chilled until ready to serve.

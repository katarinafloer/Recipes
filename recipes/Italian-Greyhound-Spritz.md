## Notes

Use gin for a more botanical flavor or vodka for a cleaner, more neutral cocktail.

Campari will create a more bitter spritz, while Aperol will make the drink sweeter and less bitter.

For decorative ice cubes, freeze fresh edible marigold flowers in an ice mold with water. Make sure the flowers are food-safe and have not been treated with pesticides.

## Instructions

1. Fill a glass with ice.
2. Add the gin or vodka, fresh grapefruit juice, and Campari or Aperol.
3. Top with sparkling water.
4. Stir gently until combined.
5. Garnish with an edible marigold flower and serve immediately.

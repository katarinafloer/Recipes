## Notes

Use wooden chopsticks as guides when slicing the sweet potatoes to prevent cutting completely through them.

Cutting vertically on one side and diagonally on the other creates the accordion shape.

Save the sweet potato offcuts and roast them separately to avoid waste.

Serve immediately while hot for the crispiest texture.

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F) fan-forced and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
2. Peel the sweet potatoes and trim the tops, bottoms, and sides to create flat edges.
3. Slice each sweet potato lengthwise into 3-4 evenly sized thick pieces.
4. Place one sweet potato slice between two wooden chopsticks.
5. Make thin vertical cuts across the sweet potato, using the chopsticks to prevent the knife from cutting completely through.
6. Flip the sweet potato slice over and make thin diagonal cuts across the opposite side.
7. Repeat with the remaining sweet potato slices and arrange them on the prepared baking tray.
8. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, sea salt, black pepper, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, and rosemary.
9. Brush the seasoned olive oil generously over the sweet potatoes, making sure the oil gets between the cuts.
10. Bake for 30 minutes, or until the side facing upward is golden brown.
11. Flip the sweet potatoes and bake for another 20 minutes, or until golden and crispy.
12. Transfer to a serving plate and garnish with fresh parsley.
13. Serve immediately while hot and crispy.
